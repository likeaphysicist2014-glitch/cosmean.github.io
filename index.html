About COSMEAN

COSMEAN is an open platform to explore physics and the cosmos. It aims to provide:

  • Historical precedents and scientific discoveries
  • Clear explanations and multi-dimensional perspectives
  • Research summaries and data insights
  • Community collaboration for physicists, students, and enthusiasts

Stay updated with latest research, experiments, and findings in physics and related fields. COSMEAN encourages:

  • Summaries of research papers and trials
  • Opportunities to collaborate and contribute
